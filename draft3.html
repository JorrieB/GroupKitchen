About Us

Our names are Brendan, Laura, Katie, and Jorrie, and we're all students in computer science at MIT. We were inspired by Jorrie's uncle, who runs a soup kitchen, to try to increase the quantity of food donations to soup kitchens by making the donation process easier and more efficient for restaurants and caterers. We'd love to hear your feedback at groupkitchen@mit.edu!
